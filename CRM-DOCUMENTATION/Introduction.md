# Introduction

This project is meant to be freely distributed under MIT License as a modern Enterprise level CRM + PM Software for organizations sized 10-1000 people in number. It will offer standard modern CRM and PM software features, some of which include:

### Customer/Prospects ticket management system: 

  Allows all ananymous and registered customers to submit tickets to a configured ticket management system. The customer-support, marketing and sales teams will have access to a complete suite of standard ticket-mamangement tools to organize, assign and respond to customers and prospective customers whether it be technical support, sales support or general enquiries.
 

### Corporate Documents Online Repository:

  This CRM + PM software will come with a versatile Document management system. This system will handle all attachments/document uploads carried out with external tickets-management-system, tasks-management-system and customer-management-system, as well as allow for a complete corporate document repository to be set up.

      - The repository documents will have permissions based on:
        1) department level authorization
        2) work-space level authorization
        3) individual-permission access to named personal

      - The documents will always be managed in a dedicated assets management server environment equivalent to AWS S3 Buckets. In house asset-sharing environments can also be coordinated as integrations.

      - All documents attached in any app of the CRM + PM software will automatically be added to the repository.

      - Our development pipeline also expects to develop an e-document creation dunctionality like an online-wiki, which would be fully incorporated with the document repository. (Not part of MVP).


### Marketing/Sales Dept. Template Documents Library:
  
  The document repository will also allow for sales/marketing to setup templates for quick access while interacting with customers.


### Customer Portal and full Profile Management System:

  All customers can be signed on to the CRM Customer Portal which can allow for custom features required by the organization like ticket creation, appointment setup and management, documents sharing and delivery, etc.
 
### Organization Full Tasks Management system: 

  Since customer inquiries are referred to as Tickets, internal organizational tools for assigning and managing tasks are termed "Tasks" in our CRM.

  The CRM + PM Software will allow for full, standard Tasks management system, allowing for work-space setup and team leader and team member functionality. The work spaces will be versatile allowing for versatile department/individuals to be associated with any work-space, and permissions handling setup by work-space creators.
 

### Top Management Overview Screens for CRM stats (Future development):

  Once the CRM + PM Software has matured we plan on adding a complete reporting feature allowing top-management to view various stats and current state of the organization as represented by the CRM and PM software. This module may take a one-two years post-launch to dveelop.


# What's different in Dotz CRM + PM Software?

Our software offers the following unique selling points, we beleive will attract its own customer base:

1) ** Inhouse deployment: ** Dotz CRM + PM software is not a cloud SaaS. We offer the ability to host the CRM in house with full data-control and access. Web Dotz and its affiliates will never have access to your company data, without prior access/approval.

This often is advantageous to organizations concerned about privacy and data-management. While also allowing full control over server-uptime as necessary for your team.

2) ** Data Safety: ** as mentioned, Dotz CRM + PM Software will not share your data with anyone - including Web Dotz and its affilliates - without your prior consent. This can be valuable for organizations seeking control of their internal data.

3) ** Customization + Dependable Support: ** Web Dotz will sign on to a minimum two year support with your organization, allowing for dedicated, enterprise level support. We can add custom features your team(s) require, and work out plans for any integrations essential to your culture and internal policies.

4) ** Economaical: ** While not the main factor in enterpise decisions, Dotz CRM + PM Software is Open Source, thus also economical. Our enterpise packages will be a fraction of the cost of our competitors, while offering continued savings for years to come, in server costs due to Web Dotz signature performant application layer.
